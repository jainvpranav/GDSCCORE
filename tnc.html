Terms & Conditions

Welcome to the GDSC (Google Developer Student Clubs) Core Team Selections! We're excited that you're considering joining our club and contributing to our mission of fostering a collaborative and innovative tech community. Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before applying for membership. By applying, you agree to adhere to these terms and conditions.

  • Commitment
    As a member of the GDSC Core Team, you are expected to actively participate and contribute to the club's activities, events, and projects. This includes attending regular meetings, workshops, and team discussions. The minimum commitment period is one academic year.
  • Zero Tolerance for Harassment
    GDSC is committed to maintaining an inclusive and respectful environment for all members. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of harmful behavior will not be tolerated. All members are expected to treat each other with kindness, respect, and professionalism.
  • Collaboration and Problem-Solving
    Core Team members are expected to work collaboratively with other team members. This involves effective communication, sharing ideas, and contributing constructively to discussions. Problem-solving skills and a willingness to brainstorm creative solutions are essential.
  • Open to Constructive Criticism
    Members of the GDSC Core Team should be open to receiving constructive criticism. Feedback is vital for personal growth and the improvement of team dynamics and projects. Embracing feedback and adapting accordingly will contribute to a positive and productive environment.
  • Responsible Conduct
    All Core Team members are representatives of GDSC and are expected to conduct themselves in a responsible and professional manner during club-related activities and online interactions. This includes maintaining a positive online presence and upholding the reputation of the club.
  • Attendance and Communication
    Regular attendance at meetings, workshops, and events is crucial for the smooth functioning of the Core Team. If you are unable to attend a meeting or event, timely communication with team leads or coordinators is required.
  • Active Participation
    Participation goes beyond attendance. Active involvement in planning, organizing, and executing events and projects is expected. Each member is encouraged to bring their unique skills and ideas to contribute to the success of GDSC.
  • Removal from the Core Team
    Failure to comply with these terms and conditions may result in the removal of a member from the Core Team. This includes but is not limited to violations of the zero-tolerance harassment policy, consistent absence without valid reasons, and disruptive behavior.
  • Confidentiality
    Certain discussions and information within the Core Team may be confidential. Members are expected to respect the confidentiality of such information and not share it with external parties without proper authorization.
  • Code of Conduct
    All members are expected to abide by GDSC's official Code of Conduct, which outlines the expected behavior, values, and guidelines for participation in the club.
